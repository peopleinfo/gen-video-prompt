## Quick Reference: Prompt Formula Template

```
[Subject with descriptive details]
[performing action]
in/on [environment/scene description]
[lighting conditions]
[style/aesthetic]
camera: [motion type]
[additional specific details]
```

## The 7 Essential Elements for Sora 2 Prompts

1. **Subject**: What/who is the main focus
2. **Action**: What's happening or moving
3. **Environment**: Where the scene takes place
4. **Lighting**: Light conditions and quality
5. **Style**: Aesthetic or visual treatment
6. **Camera Motion**: How the camera moves
7. **Details**: Specific elements that enhance realism

## Quality Checklist

- ✅ Be specific, not vague
- ✅ Include motion/action
- ✅ Specify lighting
- ✅ Define camera movement
- ✅ Set clear style/mood
- ✅ Be 20-60 words (sweet spot)
- ✅ Avoid conflicting instructions
